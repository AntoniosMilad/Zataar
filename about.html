About Zaatar

The Restaurant

Welcome to Zaatar Bistro, where the heart of the Mediterranean meets the warmth of family hospitality. As a proudly family-owned and operated establishment, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that transcends borders and tantalizes your taste buds with flavors inspired by the rich tapestry of international cuisine.

Nestled in the vibrant community of North Fresno, California, Zaatar Bistro is more than just a restaurant – it's a celebration of heritage, passion, and the joy of sharing exceptional food with those you love. Our roots run deep, drawing inspiration from the Mediterranean region, where the sun-kissed landscapes and bountiful ingredients come together to create a culinary symphony.

At Zaatar Bistro, we take pride in crafting dishes that seamlessly blend traditional Mediterranean recipes with a contemporary twist, offering a dining experience that is both familiar and exciting. Our menu showcases the finest ingredients, thoughtfully curated to bring you a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate.

But it's not just about the food; it's about the warmth of our hospitality. As a family-owned establishment, we understand the importance of creating a welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like an extension of our own family. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in the comforting embrace of our hospitality, where every detail is designed to make you feel at home.

Whether you're savoring the robust flavors of our signature dishes, enjoying a handcrafted cocktail, or sharing a meal with loved ones, Zaatar Bistro is a place where memories are made and traditions are honored. We invite you to join us at our table, where the essence of the Mediterranean comes alive in every bite, and the spirit of family is woven into the fabric of our dining experience. Thank you for choosing Zaatar Bistro – where our family's passion for exceptional food and heartfelt hospitality converge to create a dining experience that goes beyond the plate.
