About Restaurant

Welcome to Zaatar Bistro, where the heart of the Mediterranean meets the warmth of family hospitality. As a proudly family-owned and operated establishment, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that transcends borders and tantalizes your taste buds with flavors inspired by the rich tapestry of international cuisine.

Nestled in the vibrant community of North Fresno, California, Zaatar Bistro is more than just a restaurant – it's a celebration of heritage, passion, and the joy of sharing exceptional food with those you love. Our roots run deep, drawing inspiration from the Mediterranean region, where the sun-kissed landscapes and bountiful ingredients come together to create a culinary symphony.
